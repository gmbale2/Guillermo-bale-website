A career built at the intersection.

It started in São Paulo, on the agency floor at JWT Brasil briefed on the global launch of Windows 8 in partnership with Microsoft Seattle, and learning the trade by surviving it. Next came Samsung's Galaxy launches. Pirelli, HSBC, Unilever, Petrobras.

Seven years in agencies and a Cannes credit later (AfroReggae — Tá no Mapa, mapping Rio's favelas onto Google Maps), the in-house years began. Hundreds of campaigns. A 50% jump in client satisfaction. A 40% lift in agency revenue.

Then Los Angeles. Indie film marketing. Performance marketing. And eventually the inevitable founder's question — what would I build if I built it for myself?

Pro-bono mentorship for small business owners.
